# Startup Fundraising

**From first pitch to closed round — the founder’s fundraising playbook.** — built in-house by [Skill&nbsp;Me](https://skillme.dev).

Raise your round with confidence. Craft the pitch and fundraising narrative, send investor updates, understand term sheets, prep for Series A and due diligence, size the market, and model the financials.

⭐ **If this is useful, star the repo** — it's how we gauge what to build next.

## Install

- **From the catalog:** [skillme.dev/pack/startup-fundraising](https://skillme.dev/pack/startup-fundraising) — install the whole pack into Claude in one step.
- **With the skills CLI:** `npx skills add aouellets/startup-fundraising`
- **Manually:** copy any `skills/<slug>/SKILL.md` into your Claude skills directory.

## Skills in this pack

- **[VC Pitch Email](skills/vc-pitch-email/SKILL.md)** — Writes cold VC outreach emails: traction first, ask last, under 100 words.
- **[Fundraising Narrative](skills/fundraising-narrative/SKILL.md)** — Crafts the founder story arc for seed and Series A — origin, insight, traction, vision.
- **[Investor Update Writer](skills/investor-update-writer/SKILL.md)** — Monthly and quarterly investor updates: traction metrics, team highlights, ask, and forward-looking view.
- **[Term Sheet Explainer](skills/term-sheet-explainer/SKILL.md)** — Explains VC term sheet clauses in plain English with negotiation guidance.
- **[Series A Readiness](skills/series-a-readiness/SKILL.md)** — Assesses Series A readiness: metrics, narrative, team, and data room preparation.
- **[Due Diligence Checklist](skills/due-diligence-checklist/SKILL.md)** — Runs through commercial, financial, technical, and legal due diligence for investments.
- **[Market Sizing](skills/market-sizing/SKILL.md)** — Builds defensible TAM/SAM/SOM models with bottom-up and top-down triangulation.
- **[Personal Financial Planner](skills/financial-planner/SKILL.md)** — Budgeting, debt payoff, and savings planning grounded in the user's real numbers.
